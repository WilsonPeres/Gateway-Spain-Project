Running of the Bulls


For: Anyone over 18 Years old

Cost: Free

City: Pamplona

Are you ready for a activity that will have you your heart pumping and a major rush of adrenaline running through your body? If yes then you will definitely want to take part in the running of the bulls. The running of the bulls if festival activity, where many people gather around and have angry bulls chasing them for about half a mile through the narrow streets of Pamplona until they reach the arena of the bullring. It takes place at 8am during the fiestas of Sanfermin, between the 7th and the 14th of July. It is a sport and tradition that has been been around since 1592.

Must Go To Festivals

La Tomatina


For: Everyone/18+ for the after party

Cost: Packages start at 59 Euros

City: Bunol

When: Last Wednesday of August La Tomatina is one of Spain’s most famous festivals, also known as the tomato throwing festival. In this festival over 20,000 tourist and locals gather togiether each year on the last Wednesday of August to take part in a friendly battle throwing over-ripe tomatoes at each other. For over an hour you will see a battle field of people running around throwing tomatoes everywhere. Sounds like choas right? Well you are absolutly right, but it is a lot of fun and it is a tradition that has been going on since 1945. Afterwards people typically gather togiether and attend after parties for a night of fun. Going out to the many clubs and pubs and Valencia and Bunol have to offer.

Feria de Sevilla


For: Everyone

Cost: Free or have a guided tour starting at $167 USD

Location: Sevilla

When: Two weeks after Semana Santa(this year April 25th)

The Feria de Sevilla is Sevilla's spring festival. This is a week of serious dancing, drinking, eating and socialising into the late nights. The sheer extent of the April Fair's spectacle is extraordinary. The first official day Sevilla society parades around the fairground in carriages or on horseback. You will see many people dressed in traditional Spaniard outfits and dancing anywhere Sevillano music is playing. There are also daily bullfights that you can watch. There are also many rides, games, and a large ferris wheel you can ride to get a magnificent view of Sevilla. As you walk around you will see caseta's(open tents) everywhere.Inside the tents the drinks begin to flow, and tapas are served,from around 1:30pm till early next morning. Each caseta is equipped with a bar, kitchen and sound system or live entertainment playing Sevillanas.

Best Beaches

Balearic Islands

For: Everyone

Location: East of Spain on the Mediterranean Sea

The Balearic Islands, a series of islands and islets east of the Spanish mainland. The four main islands are Majorca, Ibiza, Menorca, and Formentera. Ibiza is the wild party island of the bunch, it has many clubs, bars and a crazy boat parties all year long. Majorca is the largest island, offering contrasting pleasures like all-inclusive resorts and laidback nature-oriented getaways. Menorca is a haven for the low-key traveler, entices with its archeological wonders and a variety of postcard perfect beaches. Finally theres Formentera, the tiniest island, whose ethereal beauty attracts many tourist that prefers lazy lounging by gentle crystal clear turquoise waters. There are many actities that can be done which are listed below.

Canary Islands

For: Everyone

Location: Northwest of Morroco

The Canary Islands is a series of 7 islands that belong to spain. These islands are very unique having many bio diverse landscapes such as dunes, forests, volcanoes, starry skies and above all, beaches. Some of the beaches can have black, golden, red or white sand. Most of the beaches have crystal clear water that one can swim in for days. There are many activties such as surfing, jetsking, parasailing, dolphin or whale tours, fishing, and many more that anyone can take part in. The Canary Islands are also home to Teide, the 3rd most highest active volcano in the world. If you love nature and adventure then hiking Teide is a must for you. These islands also offer all inclusive reosrts for those who want to sit back and relax. In these breath taking magical islands one will always have a great time and there is always something new to discover.
